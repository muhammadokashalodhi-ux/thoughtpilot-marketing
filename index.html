The workflow

From everyday work to industry authority — in three steps.

No content strategy degree needed. No hours lost staring at a blank page.

01

Train it on you

Add your role, industry, goals, and tone — whether that's grad student, junior dev, marketing lead, or VP. ThoughtPilot learns your voice from your profile and a short onboarding. No generic output, ever.

02

Pick a pillar, hit generate

Choose from 8 universal content pillars — career, skills, story, build-in-public, industry takes, and more. The co-pilot drafts a post grounded in live trends and your goals. Every draft is scored for voice match before it reaches you.

03

Approve, schedule, repeat

Review in the queue, tweak if needed, send to LinkedIn. The calendar handles cadence; analytics handles the scoreboard. You stay in control — no surprise auto-posts, ever.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Who is ThoughtPilot built for?
Anyone serious about LinkedIn — students hunting their first internship, grads applying for jobs, engineers, designers, founders, marketers, PMs, consultants, recruiters, and senior leaders. If you have a career or you're building one, this is for you.
How is this different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT writes generic posts. ThoughtPilot is trained on your role, industry, goals, and live trends — so output sounds like you on your best day, not a copywriter who's never met you. We score every draft for voice match before you approve.
I'm a student with no work experience. Will it work?
Absolutely. ThoughtPilot helps students post about projects, courses, internships, and learning — the exact content recruiters look for. Many users land their first role from posts generated here. There's a dedicated Student pillar built for exactly this.
Will posts actually sound like me?
Yes. The onboarding captures your tone, background, and goals. We score voice match on every draft — the target is 95%+ before you approve. You can retrain the co-pilot anytime to dial it in further.
Can it help me apply for jobs?
Yes. The AI Career Suite reviews your CV for ATS keywords, matches it against any job description with a fit score, and helps you draft outreach messages to recruiters and hiring managers — all in one place.
Do you post to LinkedIn automatically?
No — you stay in control. ThoughtPilot drafts, schedules, and analyses. You approve and publish. There are no surprise auto-posts. We believe in human-in-the-loop for your professional reputation.
Can I change or cancel my plan?
Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your dashboard. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Annual plans are refunded pro-rata if cancelled in the first 30 days.
Is my data private?
Your profile data, posts, and CV are never used to train shared models. Your voice profile belongs to you. We use enterprise-grade encryption at rest and in transit, and you can delete all your data at any time.
